### Privacy Policy for Email Transcription

**Last updated: April 29th, 2024**

Thank you for choosing Email Transcription! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

#### Information We Collect
- **User Preferences and Settings**: We store your customization settings such as your OpenAI API Key to enhance your user experience. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

#### How We Use Your Information
- **To Provide Services**: We use the stored preferences to maintain and personalize your experience with Email Transcription, ensuring that your settings are preserved across browser sessions.
- **To Improve Our Extension**: We may use non-identifiable aggregate information to help us improve the functionality and performance of our extension.

#### How We Protect Your Information
- **Data Security**: We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.
- **Data Privacy**: We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

#### Permissions Explained
- **Scripting**: Allows us to inject scripts into the Gmail website to enable transcription functionality.
- **Storage**: Used to save your settings locally on your device to preserve them across sessions.
- **Host Permissions (`https://mail.google.com/`)**: Necessary to allow the extension to operate within Gmail, facilitating the core functionality of the extension by interacting directly with Gmail pages.

#### Your Consent
By using our extension, you consent to our privacy policy.

#### Changes to Our Privacy Policy
If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. This policy was last modified on April 29th, 2024.
